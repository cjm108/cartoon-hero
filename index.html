About Triathlons

A triathlon is a multisport race with three continuous and sequential endurance races. It is made up of a Swim, then a cycle, followed by a run.

some inspirational athletes

SWIM

  1. Usually open-water
  2. In a wetsuit
  3. From 400m to 3.6km

CYCLE

  • The longest leg of the triathlon
  • Usually on a road bike
  • From 20km to 180km

RUN

  1. The last sprint to the finish line
  2. From 5km to 42km
